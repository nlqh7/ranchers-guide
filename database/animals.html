How to use this table

  • Poultry is the classic entry point: cheap animals, daily products, fast feedback.
  • Dairy trades higher startup cost for steady, higher-value goods — and dairy products are prime candidates for processing if The Ranchers ships artisan machines.
  • Livestock with multi-day cycles tends to top the profit/day charts in the mid-game, once you can afford the wait.
