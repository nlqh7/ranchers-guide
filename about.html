About The Ranchers Guide

Last updated: July 21, 2026

What this site is

theranchersguide.com is an independent, fan-made guide website for The Ranchers — the open-world ranch life sim developed by RedPilz Studio and published by Trophy Games, entering Steam Early Access on July 30, 2026. We build the things we wanted as players: clear beginner guides, honest money-making frameworks, searchable crop and animal databases, and a profit calculator that ranks anything by profit per in-game day.

How we write our guides

Our content philosophy is simple: never invent numbers. The Ranchers launches in Early Access on July 30, 2026 — until then, everything on this site is built from the free demo, official developer updates, and clearly-labeled genre knowledge. Data pages keep exact values pending until confirmed, and strategy pages teach frameworks that hold regardless of final balancing.

Our launch-week promise: when Early Access goes live, we will prioritize verified prices, growth times, and production cycles from the release build and official sources. Exact data will be marked clearly as it is confirmed.

Who it's for

Solo ranchers planning their first week, and 1–4 player co-op crews who want to split the work instead of duplicating it. If that's you, start with the beginner's guide.

Unofficial status

This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to RedPilz Studio or Trophy Games. The Ranchers, and all related game names, screenshots, artwork, and trademarks, are the property of their respective owners and are used here for informational and commentary purposes only.

Corrections & contact

Spot an error? Found a confirmed number we haven't added yet? We genuinely want to hear it — see the contact page.
