Contact Us

Last updated: July 21, 2026

The fastest way to reach the team behind theranchersguide.com is email: hello@theranchersguide.com. We read everything, and corrections get priority.

Corrections & confirmed data

Found a mistake, or sitting on a confirmed in-game number (a crop price, a growth time, an animal's production cycle) that we haven't published yet? Email us with the subject line "Correction" and, where possible, a screenshot. Confirmed data from readers is how community wikis get good — we credit contributors when they'd like credit.

Content feedback & requests

Want a guide we don't have yet — fishing routes, building layouts, NPC gift preferences? Tell us what you searched for and didn't find. Requests from real players set our writing roadmap after the Early Access launch on July 30, 2026.

Business inquiries

For advertising, partnership, or press inquiries, email hello@theranchersguide.com with the subject line "Business". Note that this is an independent fan site; for anything concerning The Ranchers game itself, please contact RedPilz Studio or Trophy Games directly.

A quick note

We're a small fan project, not a company — replies can take a few days around the launch rush. Thanks for your patience, and happy ranching.
